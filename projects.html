Projects

01

Battery Discharge Station

Overview

I am currently designing and fabricating thermal runaway test fixtures and short-circuit-protected battery discharge systems for high-voltage battery modules.

Design Highlights

Implemented precharge protection circuitry to limit inrush current on first connection. The system incorporates passive protection mechanisms against overcurrent or overvoltage conditions, along with a manual emergency stop.

Outcome

Successfully designed voltage-agnostic circuitry for battery packs of varying voltages. I am currently manufacturing multiple discharge stations that will allow the Battery Safety team to cycle batteries more efficiently and accelerate battery pack certification.
